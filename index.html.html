About Henderson & Cole Legal Chambers

Henderson & Cole Legal Chambers is a boutique commercial litigation firm located in Midtown Manhattan, New York. We represent businesses and individuals in complex disputes before the Commercial Division of the New York Supreme Court.

Our practice philosophy emphasizes aggressive advocacy, meticulous case preparation, and personalized client service. We invest the time and resources necessary to achieve optimal results in high-stakes litigation.

As a boutique firm, we maintain selective caseloads that allow us to provide each client with the attention, strategic insight, and courtroom excellence their matter deserves.
